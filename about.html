About JPN


Sushi Night

This is traditionally our biggest and most popular event!

Once a semester, we provide a variety of ingredients for over 200 people to roll their own custom-made sushi. You choose what you want in it, and we roll and cut it for you. And the best part? It's all FREE! Who doesn't love free delicious sushi?

Omatsuri

Omatsuri literally means "festival". This large event is based after traditional Japanese festivals! It is a fairly new event, but is quickly growing into JPN's big spring cultural gathering.

For an entire afternoon, we set up various booths filled with activities for people to enjoy. There are fun traditional games, Japanese trivia, and a photo booth where you can dress up in traditional Yukata and have your picture taken. At each booth, people are given a sushi ticket for participating in that activity. Rack up sushi tickets and redeem them at the food booth!


Tea Ceremony



Ever wonder what Japanese Tea Ceremonies are like? The Tea Ceremony is a traditional event that involves the ceremonial preparation and presentation of matcha, a powdered green tea. We invite a professionally trained tea master who performs the ceremony for students at WashU! Since this elaborate performance is meant to demonstrate grace and etiquette, only a few students can experience the performance at a time. Seats are limited so come early if you're interested!

Collaboration with other cultural groups



Once in awhile we collaborate with various other cultural groups on campus to bring a jointly hosted event! Come experience diverse cultural activities and interact with students who are passionate about other cultures! Some of these events include AMC Banquet and AAA Night Market. Stay posted on our facebook page or join our newsletter to hear more about these events!

Other Events

Our club is constantly changing and expanding every year. And with a larger exec board, we are always finding new events to hold for the WashU community! Various other smaller events, such as our Harajuku Halloween Night, Noodle Night study breaks, and game show night are constantly popping up as we come up with more ideas and have more resources to create fun and interative events!

As always, stay posted with our updates to hear about these awesome events!
