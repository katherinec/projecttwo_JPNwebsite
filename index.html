Our Mission

Japan Peer Network (JPN) strives to celebrate and raise awareness about Japanese culture within the Washington University community!

As a group, we provide a community for students with Japanese backgrounds or for those who are simply interested in Japan. We hold a variety of social and cultural events throughout the year to bring people who are passionate about Japanese culture together. Whether you're Japanese, an avid fan of Japanese culture, or someone who just wants to learn more about what we do, don't hesitate to contact us!

Club Members

At the beginning of every academic semester, we recruit members for our executive board. These are the people who help run the club and host our events. They are expected to attend our weekly meetings in order to stay updated on event planning. General club members who want to connect with people in the club and stay updated about our activities may simply attend our social and cultural events. More information can be found on our Facebook page, or join our newsletter to receive monthly updates!
