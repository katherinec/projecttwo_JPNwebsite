Hi there, and welcome to Katherine’s website!

I am currently a senior studying Marketing and Communications Design at Washington University in St. Louis. Although my primary area of study is in business, I have a strong interest in art and design, and I strive to incorporate my skills from both areas to create well-designed business solutions.

View my gallery! »

How do you communicate something clearly? How does an idea work for business needs? How can a design be executed effectively so that the core concept is understood and the consumer is interested? These are all questions that I tackle in my work. From sketching out a creative idea to working on product branding, problem solving is crucial in smart design. In my studio classes, each art project provides a new assignment, a new problem, and possible solutions. I am always trying to improve my skills and develop my sense in business and design.
